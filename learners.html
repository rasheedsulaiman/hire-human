AI 101—Master AI, unlock new career opportunities

Class starts Mar 1

From magic to mastery

Using AI-infused tooling feels a bit like getting access to a computer in the 80s—magic. AI 101 demystifies that magic. By the end of this course, you will be able to:

Build AI Agents

Learn how to create AI assistants that handle repetitive work and save hours each week.

Identify Tools

Discover which AI tools work best for specific tasks and when to use them.

Communicate Gains

Learn to measure AI impact and explain the business value to stakeholders.

Five modules, built for real work

Each module includes hands-on exercises designed to save you time immediately.

01

Dipping Your Toes in the Water

Watch AI agents work their magic on real work. You'll use AI to analyze thousands of data points in seconds, summarize hours of meetings, and draft professional emails—things that used to take hours.

What you'll learn

  • Leverage basic agentic concepts to create new value in your role

  • Test different use cases for automation in your role

  • Distinguish between curiosity-led ideas and business-led results

02

Establishing Your Work

Map workflows from your day and learn how to building agents to do work for you. Connect these agents to your real systems. Think: "Every time I get an email, summarize it and add action items to my task list."

What you'll learn

  • Map your workflows in order to see where the bottlenecks are

  • Prioritize work based on impact, within contexts

03

Conversation Revolutionize

Not every task is worth automating. Real results come from choosing the right opportunities. Measure the impact (savings, time, quality) and use those results to get stakeholder buy-in and funding to scale your initiatives.

What you'll learn

  • Measure the impact of automation on real work across various angles

  • Build a compelling story of the business value created

  • Craft a proposal based on the greatest need

04

Picking the Right Approach

Try different models, tools, and techniques on the same problem. Understand when simple prompts work and when you need orchestration frameworks like LangChain or custom code. Learn what "good enough" means in real deployment.

What you'll learn

  • Automate the users' work with tools outside of the basic no-code workflow

  • Measure the trade-offs for work quality

05

Consolidating Impact

Track time and money saved, measure quality and leverage those numbers to build a business case that gets you more. Package your findings into a comprehensive report to present to leadership.

What you'll learn

  • Track saved resources from data collected for impact measurement

  • Generate a case study for the impact of automation

  • Build a proposal for how you can scale your work

Capstone Project

Solve a real problem from your own work—from identifying an opportunity to measuring its impact. You'll create a full case study that proves ROI and can be used to pitch leadership, build your solution, and prepare your resume once complete. At the end, you'll present your work in a showcase event.
